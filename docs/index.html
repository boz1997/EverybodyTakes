FAQ

Good questions.

Do my guests need to download anything?

No. Guests scan the QR (or enter the 6-digit code) and the camera opens right in their browser — no account, no install. The host uses the GuestCam app to create and manage the event.

Does it work on Android?

Yes. The guest camera runs in any modern mobile browser, so Android and iPhone guests share the same gallery. On Android, the web camera is the full experience.

What is "disposable mode"?

It recreates the classic disposable camera: no preview, no retakes and no deleting, with a limited number of shots per guest — so every frame is candid and counts.

Who can see the photos?

Only people who joined your event. Knowing the event link isn’t enough — access is tied to joining. You stay in control and can remove anything from the gallery at any time.

Can I download everything afterwards?

Yes. Download individual photos and videos, select multiple, or grab the entire event as a ZIP — full resolution, no watermark.

How much does it cost?

Start free for small gatherings. Larger events are a one-time purchase per event — no subscriptions. See pricing above.
