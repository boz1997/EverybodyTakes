How it works

The host creates an event and shares a QR code or 6-character code. Guests join in seconds — no account required — and shoot photos and short videos through a shared camera. Everyone’s shots land in one collective gallery the host controls.

Your memories, your control

Hosts decide when photos are revealed (instantly, the next day, or private) and can remove any content. Guests can report or hide anything they don’t want to see. You can delete your data at any time from inside the app.
