Every guest is a photographer.

Scan a QR code, capture the night, and relive every moment together — for weddings, parties and events. Guests don't even need to install anything: the camera works right in the browser.

Try the guest camera

How it works

1. The host creates an event in the GuestCam app and shares a QR code or 6-character code.
2. Guests scan and join in seconds — no account, no download. iPhone, Android, anything with a browser.
3. Photos and videos land in one live shared gallery the host controls — like, download, relive.

No app? No problem.

Guests with the GuestCam app get the full disposable-camera experience. Everyone else captures straight from the browser — same gallery, same memories. On Android, the web camera is the experience.

Your memories, your control

Hosts decide when photos are revealed (instantly, the next day, or private) and can remove any content. Guests can report anything inappropriate — reported content is hidden immediately. You can delete your data at any time from inside the app.
